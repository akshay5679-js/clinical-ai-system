Heart disease is a condition that affects the heart and blood vessels.

Common risk factors include:
- High blood pressure
- High cholesterol
- Smoking
- Diabetes
- Obesity
- Lack of physical activity

Symptoms of heart disease may include:
- Chest pain
- Shortness of breath
- Fatigue
- Irregular heartbeat

Prevention methods include:
- Regular exercise
- Healthy diet
- Controlling blood pressure
- Avoiding smoking
- Managing stress
